Talk Check List -

Palaygo Talk Connection Checklist

  • There are a few items you need to check before engaging on Palaygo Talk. Once confirmed, please check the box and proceed to the next page. Once all boxes are checked you can proceed to the Talk session.
  • If there is any box left unchecked, you will not be able to do Palaygo Talk. In that case, please solve any issue and re-check the item.
  • You will need to go through the checklist each time you engage with Palaygo Talk. This is to reduce any problems at the actual session. We would appreciate your understanding and cooperation.

1. Do you have a Desktop PC or Note PC? (Windows or Mac)

*Currently, Palaygo Talk is not compatible with smartphones and tablets.

2. Is Chrome browser installed on your PC or Mac?

*Recommend to have the latst version of Chrome.

*Please make sure you use the Chrome browser. Currently, Palaygo Talk only works on Chrome.

Chrome can be downloaded here

3. Is there a camera and a microphone on your PC or Mac?

(External hardware works fine)

4. Do you have an earphone or a headset that can be connected to PC or Mac?

*An earphone or headset without a microphone is recommended.

*We recommend an earphone or a headset without a microphone so that the soundfiles you play for yourself on the program will not be heard by your communication partner. If your earphone or headset have a microphone included, please choose internal microphone on your PC or Palaygo Talk settings.

5. Connection test: Camera

Did you see yourself on the camera?

Recommended testing tools:

Windows - Camera

Mac - Face time

6. Connection test: Speaker and Microphone

Were you able to record your voice and play it?

Recommended testing tools:

Windows - Voice Recorder

Mac - Voice Memos

Well done!

Well done! Now you are ready to do Palaygo Talk!
